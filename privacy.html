Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps developed by Atilla Kuseyri, including ReplyAI, TaxFreeGuide, CabinCheck, MoodCast, ClipHistory, and others.

Data We Collect

Most of our apps do not collect any personal data. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or location.

AI-Powered Features

Some of our apps (including ReplyAI, ClipHistory, MoodCast, CabinCheck, TaxFreeGuide, and MeetingCost) use AI features powered by Groq, Inc. When you use these AI features:

  • The text or content you submit is sent to Groq's API for processing.
  • Groq, Inc. processes this data according to their own privacy policy, available at groq.com/privacy-policy.
  • We do not store the content you send to AI features on our own servers.
  • AI features are only activated when you explicitly request them.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Groq, Inc. — AI language model processing. Privacy policy: groq.com/privacy-policy

Local Data Storage

Some apps store data locally on your device (such as clipboard history, mood entries, or meeting records). This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at atilla@kuseyri.com.
